![[Autoboot_Utility]]# C801 U盘升级指导

前提：U盘格式须为exfat或者NTFS格式，**不支持FAT32格式**

#### 一：系统升级手顺

1、U盘新建c801\_update文件夹，将升级包、Autoboot\_Utility文件放置到U盘c801\_update文件夹
[请至钉钉文档查看附件《Autoboot\_Utility》](https://alidocs.dingtalk.com/i/nodes/jb9Y4gmKWrbqXzkdUxePRQb7WGXn6lpz?doc_type=wiki_doc&iframeQuery=anchorId%3DX02mklxq8wy4gx04xyvsad&rnd=0.09284972220753063)

![fb5084686dc548179dc6550d6e929013.png](https://alidocs.oss-cn-zhangjiakou.aliyuncs.com/res/XNkOM5j17Wd7YOY7/img/8a1ae57e-dd77-44ff-9643-77bea1b68309.png)

2、插入U盘，弹出升级界面，点击按钮进行升级

![5d5436b409184f58b0cb02c95166345d.png](https://alidocs.oss-cn-zhangjiakou.aliyuncs.com/res/XNkOM5j17Wd7YOY7/img/d60abd48-ed1a-4386-a937-6a99c10e88ce.png)

#### 二、MCU升级手顺

1、U盘新建c801\_mcu文件夹，将升级bin文件放置到U盘c801\_mcu文件夹

![image](https://alidocs.oss-cn-zhangjiakou.aliyuncs.com/res/XNkOM5j17Wd7YOY7/img/371a6a85-8da9-4396-8aed-c7b74c6aac4a.png)

2、点击进入MCU升级

![image](https://alidocs.oss-cn-zhangjiakou.aliyuncs.com/res/XNkOM5j17Wd7YOY7/img/abfc3bd6-0bce-465a-b46c-e133600f0c17.png)
